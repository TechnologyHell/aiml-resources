# 📚 Subjects Overview

This repository contains study resources, previous year question papers (PYQs), notes, lab materials, and other academic resources for **B.Tech CSE (Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning)** students of **Siliguri Institute of Technology (SIT)**, affiliated to **MAKAUT**.

> **Note**
>
> Different colleges affiliated with MAKAUT may choose different elective papers. The subject list below corresponds to the curriculum followed by **Siliguri Institute of Technology** for the 2022–2026 CSE(AI&ML) batch.
